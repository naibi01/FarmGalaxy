What NDVI value indicates healthy vegetation?

NDVI values between 0.3-0.6 indicate moderate vegetation, while 0.6-0.9 indicates dense healthy vegetation.

What is the first visible sign of nitrogen deficiency in plants?
Nitrogen is mobile in plants, so deficiencies appear first in older leaves as the nutrient is relocated to new growth.
