What weather pattern is most beneficial for wheat during its growth season?

Wheat benefits from moderate rainfall (500-800mm) and cool temperatures (15-20°C) during growth, followed by dry weather for harvest.

What is the best strategy to protect crops from unexpected frost?
Frost cloth traps ground heat and protects plants from freezing temperatures, while watering can create ice that damages plants.
